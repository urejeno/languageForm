You can study at speak English Institute on a resident visa or tourist visa. 

We also offer 3-month or 6-month student visas for people abroad or for people on a tourist visa who wish to extend their stay in the UAE for study purposes. 

If you are an international student, or a tourist looking to extend your stay in the UAE for study purposes, then click 'yes' and we will send you an email with instructions on how to apply for a student visa.
